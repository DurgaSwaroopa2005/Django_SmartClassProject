About SmartClass

SmartClass is a next-gen EdTech platform that transforms traditional learning environments into interactive digital classrooms. Our goal is to bridge the gap between teachers and students using advanced technologies like Artificial Intelligence, cloud-based content delivery, and data analytics to improve academic outcomes.

Whether you're a school administrator, a teacher, or a student, SmartClass offers a customizable, scalable, and user-friendly solution. From scheduling classes to tracking progress and generating insights, we empower education at every level.

Features include live video classes, smart whiteboards, attendance tracking, quiz creation, auto-grading, feedback collection, and much more — all accessible from web or mobile.
